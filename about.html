About us

Vision

Over the course of human history Science, Has developed from our early understanding of fire ,wind ,water, and earth

To exploring Everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life its

We believe Science should not be confined To the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is way we have over 100 different Exhibits on the many varied subjects of science To explore.

Many of these exhibits are designed For you to interact with and play around to see Science come to life

(Apart the dinosaur exhibits -the only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a Lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.
